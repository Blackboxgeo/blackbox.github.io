Episode 1: Women in Soil Ecology

Release Date: 2023-04

We invited Franciska de Vries to talk with us about "Women in Soil Ecology".

Episode 2: The Lost Art of Taxonomy

Release Date: 2023-04

We invited Zoe Lindo and Toke Høye to talk with us about "The Lost Art of Taxonomy". We are especially interested in the role of both taxonomists and machine learning approaches, how they do and will interact, and what is needed today and in the future to fulfill the demands and develop new approaches.

Episode 3: Ecological Theory across Disciplines

Release Date: 2023-05

We invited Madhav (Maddy) Thakur to talk with us about "Ecological Theory across Disciplines".

Episode 4: Synthesizing Zombie Data

Release Date: 2023-06

We invited Jérome Mathieu to talk with us about "Synthesizing Zombie Data". We talk about his current work on a soil fauna database, how this project started at his institute, and related ongoing and future projects.
